Here you can learn about car and make it go to work or go around the world!

  • Press "c" to find out what car model you have!
  • Press "m" to find out the car's model!
  • Press "d" to find out your car's mileage!
  • Press "h" to honk the horn!
  • Press "w" to go to work.
  • Press "t" to take a trip around the world.

Be sure to keep an eye on your check engine light!
